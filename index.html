    I'm a former design engineer who found a true passion in robotics. I'm the architect who brings machines to life through precise design and intelligent programming. It feels like a form of modern magic, where I serve as the magician animating lifeless hardware through logic and innovation.

It's my favorite source of wild ideas and storytelling that pushes the limits.
